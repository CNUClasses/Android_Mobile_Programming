Instructions for cleaning (from Stack Overflow )
  1. Click on Gradle pane,found in the top-right corner of Android Studio
  2. Click on your project,then Tasks -> build ->clean
In order to compile your project I will;
  1. Unzip it
  2. In Android Studio I will choose File->New->Import Project and then select your settings.gradle file. (This will rebuild iml and idea files that work on my install of Android Studio)

  • Check Permissions, especially in terms of gradle settings and test targets, you want the latest SDKs ? Then please implement runtime permissions
  • Perform error checks! Do not dereference null objects.
  • Test on a clean install!
  • Use preference change listeners rather than fetch the pref whenever its needed
